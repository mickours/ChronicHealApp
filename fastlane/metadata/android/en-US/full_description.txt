ChronicHeal is an open-source, privacy-first mobile application designed to help individuals managing chronic conditions track their health journey. Unlike traditional apps, ChronicHeal stores all your data locally, requires no account, and puts you in complete control of your medical history.

Key Features:
- Comprehensive Tracking: Log pain, symptoms, medications, meals, sleep, and more.
- Visual History: Monthly calendar view with heatmaps and detailed interactive timeline.
- Advanced Analytics: Visualize progress with charts and find correlations.
- PDF Reporting: Generate professional medical reports to share with healthcare providers.
- Local-Only AI: Optional on-device meal analysis and voice logging.
- Privacy & Security: Local storage only, biometric lock, no account needed.
- Interactive Human Silhouette: Quickly log pain locations with a body scan.
- Smart Reminders: Stay on track with medication and activity alerts.

ChronicHeal empowers you to understand your health better while keeping your sensitive data private.
